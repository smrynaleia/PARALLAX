# Instagram Follower Analysis and Account Clean‑Up (2026)

## Purpose
Use this guide to audit your follower base, identify low‑value or risky accounts, and keep your community healthy without harming reach or engagement.

## Türkçe Özet: Takipçi Analizi + Temizleme (2026)
Bu bölüm, takipçi analizi yapmak ve güvenli şekilde takipçi/etkileşim temizliği uygulamak isteyenler için Türkçe bir özet sunar.

**Takipçi analizi için ölçülecekler**
- Toplam takipçi ve takip edilen sayısı
- Son 30 günlük erişim ve etkileşim oranı
- Son 90 günde en çok etkileşim alan 10 paylaşım
- İnaktif/şüpheli hesap tahmini

**Düşük kaliteli takipçi sinyalleri**
- Profil fotoğrafı yok
- Kullanıcı adı otomatik üretilmiş gibi (rastgele sayı/harf)
- Çok az paylaşım veya sadece yeniden paylaşım
- Kopya/kalıp yorumlar
- Takipçi–takip edilen oranı aşırı dengesiz
- 6–12 aydır görünür aktivite yok

**Güvenli temizlik yaklaşımı**
- Günlük 20–50 hesapla sınırlı kal
- Açıkça bot/spam olanlarla başla
- Etkileşim alan gerçek hesapları kaldırmaktan kaçın

**Otomatik unfollow uygulamaları hakkında**
- Instagram, otomasyon ve şifre isteyen üçüncü taraf uygulamalara karşı katıdır.
- Toplu unfollow botları hesabın kısıtlanmasına veya kapatılmasına yol açabilir.
- Bu yüzden otomatik unfollow yerine, Instagram’ın kendi araçlarıyla kademeli temizlik önerilir.

## What to Track (2026‑ready)
Capture a snapshot of your account for a clear baseline before making changes.

**Account health**
- Total followers
- Following count
- Accounts reached (last 30 days)
- Engagement rate (per post + overall)
- Top 10 posts by engagement (last 90 days)

**Follower quality**
- Estimated inactive or ghost followers
- Suspected bots or suspicious accounts
- Followers with zero or low engagement history

**Community signals**
- Most frequent commenters
- Most frequent story viewers
- High‑value followers (creators, brands, partners)

## Data Sources You Can Use
Instagram limits direct exports of follower details. Use the best mix of what you can access:

- **Instagram Insights** (professional accounts): reach, engagement, audience demographics.
- **Account Center** (download your information): follower and following lists.
- **Manual reviews**: profile signals, activity recency, content quality.
- **Trusted analytics platforms** (if used): avoid tools that require your password or violate IG policies.

## Red‑Flag Signals for Low‑Quality Followers
These indicators are common in spam or bot accounts:

- No profile photo
- Username looks auto‑generated (random numbers/letters)
- Few posts or all reposts
- Generic or copy‑paste comments
- Abnormal following/follower ratios (e.g., 7k following, 23 followers)
- Profile language doesn’t match your audience or content
- No visible activity for 6–12 months

## Clean‑Up Strategy (Safe + Gradual)
Avoid sudden changes that can trigger engagement drops or account flags.

**Step 1: Segment your followers**
- **High‑value**: frequent engagement or business relevance.
- **Medium‑value**: occasional engagement, real‑looking profiles.
- **Low‑value**: inactive or suspicious.

**Step 2: Remove or restrict in batches**
- Remove 20–50 accounts per day maximum.
- Prioritize obvious bots or spam.
- Avoid removing engaged accounts even if they seem inactive.

**Step 3: Review following list**
- Unfollow accounts that don’t align with your niche or are inactive.
- Keep your following list intentional (signals relevance to the algorithm).

## Engagement Recovery Plan (After Clean‑Up)
Strengthen your engagement signals after removing accounts.

- Post consistently (3–5 posts/week).
- Use polls, Q&As, and interactive stickers in Stories.
- Reply to top comments quickly for the first 60 minutes.
- Encourage saves and shares with practical tips or checklists.

## Monthly Maintenance Checklist
Repeat light clean‑up monthly to avoid drastic drops.

- Review 50–100 low‑value accounts.
- Remove 10–30 obvious bots.
- Refresh Story highlights to keep new followers engaged.
- Track engagement rate changes.

## Compliance + Safety Notes
- Do not use mass‑unfollow bots or automation tools.
- Avoid third‑party apps that ask for your password.
- Follow Instagram’s Terms of Use to prevent account penalties.
